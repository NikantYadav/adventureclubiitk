How to Join

To enjoy climbing at IITK climbing wall, one needs to obtain a membership card from the front desk at the New SAC (in front of the new gym). The current membership charges are*:

  • 1 Day: Rs 20/-
  • 1 Month: Rs 100/-
  • 3 Months: Rs 200/-

*Rates are subject to change without prior notice.

Coach

Mr. Pushpender Singh is a highly qualified and skilled climber who has a lot of experience teaching first timers and turning them into proficient climbers. Safety of the climbers is the first priority and Mr. Pushpender always makes sure of that.

Timings

Monday to Saturday: 6:00pm to 8:00pm (subject to variation)

Sunday: Closed
